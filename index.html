How it works

1

Pick a fight to score

Browse upcoming and live fights on the Fight Night screen. Tap Score Now when the first bell rings.

2

Score each round

Use the +/- buttons to award rounds to each fighter. Lock your score before the next round starts.

3

Submit your scorecard

When the final bell rings, submit your card. Real judges can't change their scores — neither can you.

4

See how you compare

Your scorecard joins thousands of others. See the community consensus, read comments, and defend your card.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

WHAT WE COLLECT

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Display name, username, bio and profile photo (optional, set by you)
  • Scorecards and round scores you submit
  • Comments and ratings you post
  • Device information for app functionality

WHAT WE DON'T COLLECT

  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not share your personal information with anyone outside of providing the service

HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display your scorecards and profile to the community (only what you choose to make public)
  • To calculate leaderboard rankings and judge tiers
  • To send notifications you have opted into

DATA STORAGE

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest.

YOUR RIGHTS

  • You can edit or delete your profile at any time from the app
  • You can withdraw submitted scorecards at any time
  • You can request full deletion of your account and data by emailing us

CONTACT

For any privacy queries: privacy@roundcard.app

CHANGES

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
