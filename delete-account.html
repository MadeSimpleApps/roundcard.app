Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Here's how to request deletion of your account and data.

Option 1 — Delete in the app

1

Open RoundCard

Launch the app on your device and make sure you're logged in.

2

Go to Profile

Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen.

3

Tap Edit Profile

Scroll down to find the Delete Account option at the bottom of the Edit Profile screen.

4

Confirm deletion

Confirm your decision. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you can't access the app, email us directly and we'll delete your account within 30 days.

Email Us to Delete Account →

What gets deleted

  • Your account and profile information
  • All scorecards you have submitted
  • All comments and ratings you have posted
  • Your followers and following relationships
  • Your badges, streaks and stats
  • All notifications associated with your account

What we retain

We may retain anonymised, aggregated data (such as fight scoring statistics) that cannot be linked back to you personally. This data helps us improve the app experience for all users.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your scorecards and data will be removed and cannot be recovered.

Processing time

Deletion requests made in the app are processed immediately. Email requests are processed within 30 days of receipt.

Contact

For any questions about account deletion or your data, contact us at privacy@roundcard.app
