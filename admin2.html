How Scores Are Calculated
RoundCard uses real fan scorecards to determine community verdicts.
Who Scored

Every score comes from a real registered user scoring live in RoundCard using the 10-point must system.

Who Won the Round

Strictly more than 50% of judges must have a fighter winning. Ties and 50/50 splits default to 10-10.

By What Score

The most commonly submitted score among the majority winner's judges. Naturally reflects knockdowns (10-8) and deductions.

Why Numbers May Differ

Community Consensus — % with each fighter winning overall.
Viewers Verdict — built round by round from majority winners. Both valid, can differ.
