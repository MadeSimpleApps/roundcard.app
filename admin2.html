How Scores Are Calculated
RoundCard uses real fan scorecards to determine community verdicts.
Who Scored

Every score comes from a real registered user scoring the fight live in RoundCard, round by round, using the same 10-point must system as the official judges. Scores are locked after each round.

Who Won the Round

Strictly more than 50% of judges must have a fighter winning for them to win the round. Anything else — ties, three-way splits, 50/50 — defaults to 10-10.

By What Score

From the majority winner's judges, the most commonly submitted score is used. Naturally accounts for knockdowns (10-8) and deductions (9-9).

Why Numbers May Differ

Community Consensus — % of judges who have each fighter winning their overall scorecard.
Viewers' Verdict — built round by round from majority winners. These can tell different stories; both are valid.
